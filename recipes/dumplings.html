Description

Dumplings are a Chinese treasure, with a sweet and savory inside along with a soft breaded outside, it's a savory treat for the whole family to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 4 Teaspoons Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • Stew or Soup, For Cooking and Serving

Steps

  1. Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Add the milk and mix. The batter should be thick enough to be scooped and dropped from a spoon. Let it rest for a few minutes.
  3. Once the boiling stew or soup is nearly done, drop spoonfuls of the batter on top. Be sure that there is plenty of liquid in the pot, but try to keep the dumplings on top of the vegetables and meat.
  4. Cover and cook for about 10 to 15 minutes, depending on the size of the dumplings. Do not remove the lid until it is time to check the dumplings; when ready, a toothpick inserted into one of the dumplings should come out clean.
  5. Once done, spoon the stew or soup with the dumplings into a bowl and enjoy.
