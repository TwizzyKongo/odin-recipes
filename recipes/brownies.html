Description

Hot fudge brownies are a staple american dessert, made by beloved grandmas across the nation. It's not that healthy, but it makes up for it in deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Stick Melted Butter
  • 1 Cup Cocoa
  • 2 Tablespoon Hot Fudge Sauce (Extra Thick)
  • 3 Tablespoons Hot Fudge Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Cup Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Lightly grease 9x9 pan with cooking spray or butter.
  3. In a large bowl, combine sugar and butter and mix until soft.
  4. Add vanilla and eggs, and mix thoroughly.
  5. Fold in flour and cocoa, mix well but do not overmix.
  6. Mix in 2 tbsp lightly warmed hot fudge.
  7. Mix well.
  8. Pour into pan.
  9. Spread rest of the hot fudge across top of brownies I make 3 long lines, then drag a spoon back and forth over the top of the brownies to make a design in the fudge.
  10. Bake until sides of brownies are crunchy and have slightly pulled away from the pan. Toothpick inserted in middle might not come out clean because of the hot fudge swirled on top, but if you can avoid the fudge, toothpick in middle should come out clean.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies.
